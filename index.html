Meet the Healer

You Are Not
Broken, Love.

"My intention is not to 'fix' you, because you were never broken. It is to help you feel safe enough to soften."

I created Your Healing Compass from the depths of my own healing journey. After years of moving through trauma, heartache, loss, and transformation, I began gathering the wisdom, tools, and soul remembrance that helped carry me from surviving into truly living. This space was born from that journey.

My name is Tamsyn, and while I wear many titles — intuitive healer, evidential medium, spiritual guide, Reiki practitioner, artist, earthy witch, and forever-evolving soul — at the heart of it all, I am simply someone devoted to helping others remember who they truly are.

If you've found your way here, perhaps something within you is longing for a softer path. A place where you can exhale. A place where you feel seen, supported, and gently guided back to yourself beneath the expectations, the survival patterns, and the noise of the world.

Connected enough to trust yourself again. And supported enough to step into the fullest expression of who you are.
